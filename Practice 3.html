Recipe: How to bake a cake

  1. Preheat the oven at 180°C (350°F)
  2. Mix flour, sugar, eggs and butter in a bowl.
  3. Pour the mixture into a greased cake pan.
  4. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  5. Let the cake cool before frosting or serving.
